What is GreenKeeper?

Imagine a forest sentinel, ever vigilant, protecting its leafy kingdom. That's GreenKeeper, an intelligent system that safeguards forests from the dangers of fire and illegal logging. This technological guardian weaves a web of sensors across the woodland, meticulously monitoring temperature, smoke, and even location. When danger whispers on the wind, GreenKeeper leaps into action, triggering alarms and sending precise GPS coordinates to forest rangers. Like a superhero armed with data and alerts, GreenKeeper stands guard, ready to protect the emerald heart of our world.
